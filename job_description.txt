Mô tả công việc

This is an excellent opportunity to gain hands-on experience in artificial intelligence and machine learning while working on real-world projects. As an intern, you will collaborate with experienced professionals, learn cutting-edge technologies, and contribute to meaningful AI solutions.

Work on developing and fine-tuning machine learning models and algorithms.
Preprocess, clean, and organize data for analysis and modeling.
Conduct exploratory data analysis (EDA) and create insightful visualizations.
Research emerging trends in artificial intelligence to support ongoing projects.
Test, validate, and optimize AI models to ensure high performance.
Collaborate with team members to integrate AI solutions into larger systems.
Document your work, including experiments, processes, and results.
